The KiwiSaver Growth Simulator is a structured financial modelling tool designed to project long-term retirement outcomes based on user-defined inputs such as age, income, contribution levels, and investment strategy. It integrates key variables including compounding returns, contribution rates, and fee impacts to provide a clear estimate of potential portfolio growth through to retirement age. This enables users to better understand the long-term implications of their savings and investment decisions. This tool is for informational and illustrative purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. All projections are estimates derived from historical data and assumed market conditions, and actual outcomes may vary significantly.
